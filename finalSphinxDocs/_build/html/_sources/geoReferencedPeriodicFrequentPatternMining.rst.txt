Geo-referenced periodic frequent patterns describe consistent patterns of activity or events that occur in specific geographic areas at regular time intervals.
These patterns may reveal recurring trends, behaviors, or phenomena in spatially and temporally referenced data, such as movement patterns, environmental changes, or human activities

Applications:Transportation and Logistics, Environmental Monitoring and Conservation, Urban Planning and Infrastructure Management.
